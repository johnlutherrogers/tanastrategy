MidWest US-China Association

MWCA was founded with the goal of attracting Chinese Investment into the mid-America region of the US. Former US Senator Adlai Stevenson was a founder and the initial Chairman and John Rogers served as President.

The Association sponsored and participated in conferences throughout China.
